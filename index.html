Trevor Philips is one of the three playable protagonists in Grand Theft Auto V. He is a former military pilot and a career criminal who runs his own drug trafficking operation in the fictional city of Los Santos. Trevor is known for his erratic behavior, violent tendencies, and unpredictable nature.
